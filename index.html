ABOUT THE HUB

Gaming Hub is a retro gaming sanctuary created by Siddhartha Ramrukha, dedicated to preserving the golden age of browser gaming with modern pixel-perfect aesthetics.

Our mission: deliver high-quality, free retro games that capture the essence of classic arcade gaming while being accessible on any device. No downloads, no fees, just pure nostalgic gaming bliss.

Experience the magic of 8-bit adventures, neon-lit challenges, and pixel-perfect gameplay that brings back the excitement of classic arcade halls and early computer gaming.

Founded in 2025, Gaming Hub rapidly became the go-to destination for retro gaming enthusiasts worldwide. Discover more projects at siddharthawebdev.com.
