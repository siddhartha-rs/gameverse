About Me

My Story

8Bit Island started as a simple idea in my mind: to relive the magic of classic browser games I grew up with. I'm Siddhartha Ramrukha, and I built this project to bring those nostalgic moments back to life, blending the charm of 8-bit adventures with a clean, modern design that runs flawlessly on any device.

Every pixel, every sound, every challenge in these games reflects the joy I felt discovering arcades and early computer games. My goal is to let anyone experience that same excitement‚Äîwithout downloads, without fees‚Äîjust pure, accessible retro fun.

8Bit Island is more than a collection of games; it's my personal homage to the golden age of gaming. I want players to feel the thrill of winning, the anticipation of a close match, and the simple joy of playing again and again, no matter their device or location.

Founded in 2025, this project represents my journey as a creator, a gamer, and someone passionate about preserving gaming history. Explore more of my work at siddharthawebdev.com.
